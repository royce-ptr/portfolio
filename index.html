About me

Honestly, I'm the guy friends text when their laptop won't turn on — and I kind of love that. There's something satisfying about taking a problem that's stressing someone out and quietly making it go away, whether that's a forgotten password, a dead hard drive, or software that just refuses to cooperate.

I've done that everywhere from the help desk at LA County to the Genius Bar energy of the Apple Store, so I'm just as comfortable calming down a frustrated user as I am digging into a ticket. I care a lot about getting the little things right — clear notes, accurate records, and leaving things better than I found them.

Outside of work, that same curiosity shows up in my own setup: I build and tinker with my PC, mess with hardware, and learn by breaking things and fixing them again. It keeps me sharp, and it's genuinely fun.
